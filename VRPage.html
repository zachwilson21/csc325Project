Experience
  1. Move your cursor around on the floor and click to reposition yourself.
  2. Click and drag your mouse to move your field of view.
  3. Click images and videos on the wall to view and learn more!
  4. At any point while viewing an image/video, click 'x' to go back to the gallery.
  5. Take your time, enjoy yourself, and always feel free to come back!
