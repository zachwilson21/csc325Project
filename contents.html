Chicago Music Exhange Guitar Gallery Items

This VR gallery was designed to help viewers learn about some of the unique guitars in the music industry. We hope this gallery will also show how the composition of each guitar contributes to the sound and style of each guitar. These features include the neck design, the type of wood used for the body, and the fretboard style. The group's shared interest in rock music also helped inform our decision to use the Chicaog Music Exchange as the foundation of which classic guitars to use in this gallery. The guitars featured in the gallery are shown in the order below.

  1. Fender Stratocaster Candy Apple Red 1966
  2. Gibson Les Pauk Custom 1968
  3. Fender Rosewood Telecaster Natural 1969
  4. Fender Custom Shop 1962 Jazzmaster "Chicago Special" Heavy Relic Aged Candy Apple Red w/Painted Headcap
  5. Gibson ES-335 Sunburst 1960
  6. Gibson Custom Shop Murphy Lab 1961 Les Paul SG Custom Heavy Aged Heavy Antique Ebony
  7. PRS Private Stock Custom 24 Natural Smoked Burst Koa w/Dirty Natural Smoked Burst Neck
  8. Ibanez RG8527Z J. Custom Royal Blue 2021
